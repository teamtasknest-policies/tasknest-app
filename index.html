Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: March 2026

Binding Agreement

Welcome to TaskNest. By accessing or using our platform, you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. Please read them carefully before using our services.

1. About TaskNest

TaskNest is a freelance marketplace platform that connects Task Providers (businesses or individuals who post tasks) with Task Users (freelancers who complete tasks). TaskNest operates as an unregistered business based in India and facilitates digital task-based transactions.

2. Eligibility

  • You must be at least 18 years of age to use TaskNest.
  • You must provide accurate and complete registration information.
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.
  • One person may not maintain more than one active account.

3. Platform Usage

  • Task Providers may post tasks and pay via our escrow system through Razorpay.
  • Task Users may browse, accept, and complete tasks listed on the platform.
  • TaskNest charges a platform commission on completed task payments.
  • All transactions are facilitated through our secure escrow mechanism.

4. Prohibited Activities

  • Posting fraudulent, misleading, or illegal tasks.
  • Harassing, threatening, or abusing other users.
  • Attempting to bypass the platform's payment system.
  • Sharing false reviews or manipulating the rating system.

5. Escrow & Payments

Payments for tasks are held in escrow until the Task Provider approves the completed work. Once approved, TaskNest releases the funds to the Task User after deducting the platform commission. All payments are processed via Razorpay.

6. Termination

TaskNest reserves the right to suspend or terminate any account that violates these Terms, engages in fraudulent activity, or disrupts the platform's integrity without prior notice.

7. Limitation of Liability

TaskNest is not liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of our platform, including disputes between Task Providers and Task Users.

By using TaskNest, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions.

Shipping Policy

Digital Services

TaskNest is a 100% digital platform. We do not sell, ship, or deliver any physical products.

Nature of Our Services

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Refund Guidelines

TaskNest strives to ensure fair outcomes for all parties. This policy outlines the conditions under which cancellations and refunds are handled.

Task Cancellation by Provider

  • If a Task Provider cancels a task before it is accepted, a full refund is processed to the original payment method.
  • If cancelled after acceptance but before completion, the refund is subject to review based on the work completed.
  • Cancellation requests must be submitted through the TaskNest app or via email.

Refund Eligibility

  • Refunds are processed if deliverables do not meet the stated requirements.
  • Disputes must be raised within 48 hours of task completion.
  • TaskNest admin will review disputes and make final decisions.
  • Platform commission fees are non-refundable once a task is completed.

Refund Timeline

  • Approved refunds credited to TaskNest wallet: 2–3 business days.
  • Wallet-to-bank refunds: 5–7 business days.
  • Razorpay refunds: subject to their standard policy.
For refund requests, email teamtasknest@gmail.com with your Task ID and account details.
